Ramen

Description

I absolutely dig Japanese cutlure. And I also like cooking, so when the two collide, my mind gives birth to something beautiful like the picture above.

Preparation time: 20 minutes (if you also want to marinade your meat, take those hours into account)
Cooking time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 400 grams of ramen noodles
  • 4 pork chops or 4 chicken breasts
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Chinese 5 spices (if you happen to live in Russia, you can use Kotanyi Азиатская кухня)
  • Hot chili pepper (as spices or fresh, kinda optional)
  • 4 boiled eggs (optional)
  • Worcestershire sauce (optional)
  • Sweet corn (optional)
  • Dried nori sheet (optional)
  • Green onion (optional)

Steps

  1. If you're going to marinade the meat, then use spices, garlic, ginger, soy sauce and honey for it. The trick here is to reuse the marinade for the broth later
  2. If you don't have time for the marinade, then just pour in everything I mentioned in the previous step into the kettle and bring it to the boil
  3. Add a pinch of sugar and more spices to taste
  4. While you wait for the spices in the broth to open up, start hard boiling some eggs
  5. Start frying the meat, around 5 minutes on each side
  6. Cook the noodles as per1 packet instructions
  7. Once noodles are ready, separate the noodles into 4 bowls evenly
  8. When the meat is cooked and eggs are boiled, start putting the broth, toppings, and meat into each bowl. The image above might help you
